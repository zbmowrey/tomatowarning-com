# Tomato Warning: Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Identity & Mission

**Tagline:** "Chase the storm."
**Secondary:** "Brace yourself." | "From the storm that named us." | "Born in a storm. Built for your table."
**Mission:** Tomato Warning is a premium salsa brand rated on the EF-1 to EF-5 tornado heat scale. The brand combines
extreme flavor with extreme charity, donating a percentage of net profits to named disaster relief organizations.

## 2. Voice & Tone

- The product is playful; the mission is earnest. Never mix them up.
- **Five Pillars:**
    1. Heat you can trust (reliable Scoville ranges).
    2. Flavor first, always.
    3. A story worth telling.
    4. Buy a jar, fund recovery.
    5. Made for this region (Gulf Coast FL & Tornado Alley).

## 3. Visual Identity

- **Color Palette:** Storm charcoal base, premium white, accent gold (primary interactive), radar teal (secondary).
- **EF Gradient:** EF-1 warm amber → EF-2 deep orange → EF-3 rust red → EF-4 dark crimson → EF-5 near-black.
- **Typography:** Bold condensed sans or slab for display (authoritative, weather-alert style). Clean grotesque for
  body. No script fonts.
- **Imagery:** The "Tornado Pepper" logo mark (tomato morphing into a funnel), doppler radar texture, isobar/pressure
  lines. *No generic southwestern aesthetics (no cactus, no desert palette).*
- **Labels:** Sleek storm charcoal, thin metallic gold foil bands at top and bottom.
- **Vibe:** Premium, modern, authoritative, dynamic.

## 4. The EF Scale System

| Level | Name         | Color        | Scoville Range       | Heat Descriptor                                                    |
|-------|--------------|--------------|----------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------|
| EF-1  | Coastal Calm | Warm amber   | 500-1,500 SHU        | A gentle gust — big flavor, little fire                            |
| EF-2  | Gulf Breeze  | Deep orange  | 1,500-5,000 SHU      | Building on the horizon — smoke, depth, and your first real tingle |
| EF-3  | Squall Line  | Rust red     | 5,000-25,000 SHU     | The storm is here — tropical sweetness chased by real heat         |
| EF-4  | Supercell    | Dark crimson | 25,000-100,000 SHU   | Rotation confirmed — deep, relentless, rewarding                   |
| EF-5  | Ground Zero  | Near-black   | 100,000-500,000+ SHU | Total devastation — for the few who dare                           |
