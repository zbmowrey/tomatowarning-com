# P1-20: Build Product Pages (5 Varieties)

"Coming Soon" product pages for all five EF levels. Each page creates desire and captures email signups.

## Page Template (per variety)
- Rendered jar mockup
- Product name and EF level
- Flavor headline and heat descriptor
- Scoville range commitment
- Two key ingredients highlighted
- "Notify Me When Available" email capture (consumer list)
