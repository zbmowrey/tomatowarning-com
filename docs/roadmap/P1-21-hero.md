# P1-21: Build Hero Section — "The EF System"

The most critical section of the site. Every visitor sees it. Communicates the brand's primary differentiator in under 5 seconds.

## Design Concept
- Horizontal EF-scale bar: amber (EF-1) to near-black (EF-5)
- Rendered jar at each level
- Headline: "Finally, a heat scale that means something."
- Sub-copy: "EF-1 (mild) to EF-5 (extreme) — every jar Scoville-rated, every batch consistent."
- CTA below fold to origin story / mission page

## Dependencies
- P1-07: Visual identity (color system, typography)
- P1-13: Brand voice (headline copy)
- P1-15: Product mockup renders (five jars displayed)

## Notes
- This is where the brand lives or dies in the first 5 seconds
- Must be compelling even without interactive elements (static first, animated later in Phase 2)
- Render quality is critical — if renders feel like placeholders, the hero fails
