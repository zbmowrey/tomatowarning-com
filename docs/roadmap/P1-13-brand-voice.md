# P1-13: Define Brand Voice & Messaging

Create the brand voice guide that governs all content creation across the site and beyond.

## Governing Principle
"The product is playful; the mission is earnest. Never mix them up."

## Five Messaging Pillars
1. **Heat you can trust** — EF scale solves a real problem
2. **Flavor first, always** — heat is real but not a stunt
3. **A story worth telling** — the origin is genuine
4. **Buy a jar, fund recovery** — specific, named, reported
5. **Made for this region** — Gulf Coast FL is home

## Deliverables
- Brand voice guide document
- Tone spectrum with examples: product pages (playful), mission page (earnest), social media (conversational)
- Do/don't examples for each context
- Persona-specific tone notes (Marcus = flavor-forward, Jennifer = credibility-focused, Coach Dave = practical)
