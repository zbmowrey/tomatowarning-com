# P1-01: Establish Legal Entity & Foundational Insurance

Form LLC (or chosen entity), draft operating agreement, obtain product liability insurance. Publix requires product liability insurance for vendor applications. Cannot enter retail contracts or co-packer agreements without a legal entity.

## Deliverables
- Registered business entity
- Operating agreement
- Product liability insurance policy
- EIN for tax purposes
