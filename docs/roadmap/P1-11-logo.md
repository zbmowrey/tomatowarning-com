# P1-11: Design Logo & Vortex Mark

Design the brand logo featuring a stylized vortex mark. Must work at 16px (favicon) to embroidery scale.

## Requirements
- Stylized vortex/tornado mark — not clip art, a precision brand mark
- Must incorporate the established color palette from P1-07
- Scalable: favicon (16px) → social avatar → jar label → embroidered hat → signage
- Works in single-color (for print/embroidery)
- Wordmark "TOMATO WARNING" paired with vortex mark

## Deliverables
- Logo in SVG, PNG (multiple sizes), favicon
- Print-ready formats (CMYK, high-res)
- Single-color variants (black, white, reversed)
- Brand mark usage guide (minimum size, clear space, prohibited modifications)

## Notes
- Blocked by P1-07 (visual identity — color palette must be final)
- Blocks P1-15 (product renders — labels need the logo)
- Commission a branding-focused designer, not a general graphic designer
- The mark should suggest a vortex without being a literal tornado illustration
