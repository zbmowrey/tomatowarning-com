# P1-14: Capture Founder Content (Photo/Video)

Capture founder photography and optional video for the mission page. A faceless brand with a personal origin story will feel inauthentic.

## Deliverables
- Professional founder headshot/portrait
- Candid/lifestyle photo (family context, Gulf Coast setting)
- Optional: short video clip for mission page (30-60 seconds, origin story)

## Notes
- Gap item: mission page credibility depends on a real face behind the story
- Can start anytime — no blocking dependencies
- A high-quality phone shoot is acceptable if professional photography is not immediately available
