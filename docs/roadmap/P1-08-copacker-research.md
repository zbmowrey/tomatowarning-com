# P1-08: Research Co-Packer Options (Gulf Coast FL)

Identify and evaluate co-packers in the Gulf Coast FL region. Not blocking the branding site but critical for validating pricing, Scoville commitments, and minimum runs.

## Deliverables
- List of 3-5 Gulf Coast FL co-packers with capabilities
- Minimum run sizes and per-unit pricing
- Timeline from recipe to first batch
- Capability assessment: can they handle ghost pepper / Carolina Reaper safely?
- Scoville testing capability (or third-party lab recommendation)

## Notes
- Gap item: without co-packer economics, the price points and giving percentages on the site are theoretical
- Informs P1-09 (Scoville validation) and P1-03 (giving economics)
- Critical path to any Phase 2 work
