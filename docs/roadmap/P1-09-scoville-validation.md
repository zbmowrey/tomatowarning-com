# P1-09: Validate Scoville Ranges with Recipe Developer

Confirm that the published SHU ranges for each EF level are achievable and consistent in production.

## Ranges to Validate
| EF Level | Target SHU | Key Peppers |
|----------|-----------|-------------|
| EF-1 | 500-1,500 | Poblano |
| EF-2 | 1,500-5,000 | Chipotle, serrano |
| EF-3 | 5,000-25,000 | Habanero |
| EF-4 | 25,000-100,000 | Ghost pepper |
| EF-5 | 100,000-500,000+ | Carolina Reaper, Trinidad Moruga Scorpion |

## Deliverables
- Food scientist or recipe developer consultation
- Confirmed achievable ranges per EF level
- Batch consistency assessment
- Recommended testing methodology for production QC

## Notes
- The EF system's credibility rests on Scoville consistency
- Publishing ranges that cannot be delivered collapses the brand's primary differentiator
- Must precede any consumer-facing Scoville commitments on the site
