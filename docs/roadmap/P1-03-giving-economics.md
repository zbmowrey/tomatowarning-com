# P1-03: Model Giving & Fundraising Economics

Build a financial model confirming the giving percentage and fundraising partner margins are viable at various volume levels.

## Deliverables
- Spreadsheet model: COGS per jar, margin at $7.99-$9.99 retail, net profit at various volumes
- Giving percentage viability: 5% of net profits at Year 1 volumes vs. Year 3 volumes
- Fundraising channel economics: 50% margin to organizations — does it work with co-packer pricing?
- Break-even analysis per channel (DTC, retail, fundraising)

## Notes
- Gap item: publishing unmodeled giving percentages on the site is a liability
- Informs P1-02 (charity partner) — the percentage commitment depends on this model
- Informs P2-02 (fundraising program) — margin structure must be validated here
