# P1-18: Build Nonprofit CTA Page — "Fundraise for Recovery"

Landing page for nonprofit/fundraising partners (Coach Dave persona).

## Page Content
- Headline: "Your next fundraiser."
- Three clear bullets: 45-50% margin, no upfront cost, custom label option
- Simple interest form (org name, contact, org type, location, estimated campaign size)
- Nonprofit mailing list opt-in
- The dual-mission pitch: "Your community raises money for your cause, and every jar sold also helps fund disaster recovery."

- P1-13: Brand voice (copy tone)
