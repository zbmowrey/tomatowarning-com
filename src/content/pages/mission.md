## The EF Rating System

Our sauces are rated using the Enhanced Fujita (EF) scale — the same system meteorologists use to classify tornadoes. It's our way of being honest about heat while honoring the culture that inspired us.

| Level | Name | Scoville Range | Character |
|-------|------|---------------|-----------|
| EF-1 | Coastal Calm | 500–1,500 | A gentle gust |
| EF-2 | Gulf Breeze | 1,500–5,000 | Building on the horizon |
| EF-3 | Squall Line | 5,000–25,000 | The storm is here |
| EF-4 | Supercell | 25,000–100,000 | Rotation confirmed |
| EF-5 | Ground Zero | 100,000–500,000 | Total devastation |
